The projector — on, lamp blazing

The same mini‑LED body, lit. Pointed straight at the viewer, the lens is blown out — light bleeds over the bezel and spills onto the body. The plus aperture is still there, still subtle, but now because the glare washes its edges rather than because it’s dark.

On — pointed at you, blown out with light bleed
