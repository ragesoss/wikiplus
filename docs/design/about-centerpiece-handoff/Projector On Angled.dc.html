The projector — on, yawed at eye level

Wider faceplate and a larger lens, matching the face‑on proportions. A rounded‑rect prism (faceplate = cross‑section), pure yaw at eye level — no top, a curved side wall, a back leg just visible, and the elliptical lens projecting the blown‑out plus toward you and a touch right.

On, yawed — face-on proportions, big elliptical lens

Faceplate proportions and lens size now track the face‑on design. Tunable: yaw amount, side‑wall depth, lens ellipse ratio, beam spread.
