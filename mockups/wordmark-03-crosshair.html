03 · Crosshair seam

The “+” is architecture, not a glyph: the header’s (thickened) bottom rule is the horizontal stroke, and a short indigo vertical stroke rising from the column divide completes a “+” precisely at the seam. The wordmark itself is just the two flanking words, their gap straddling the cross. Left (Wiki): restrained serif “wiki”. Right (Plus): indigo Source Sans 900 “plus”. The cross is drawn by the page’s own structure — the two rules that define the two worlds intersect to make the mark.
