wiki+ Topic view — design exploration (round 3)

Ten directions for the main article view. All render the real Wikipedia “Photosynthesis” article alongside 14 real, verified videos (YouTube long-form, YouTube Shorts, and TikTok) curated by creators. Each clip carries a context note that separates fact from the creator’s opinion/level, flags accuracy, and links to the relevant article section. This round adds real embeds/thumbnails and explores how each layout scales past what’s visible (overview · see-more · load-more · infinite scroll · pagination — the topic implies 47 clips, of which 14 are the curated top page).

One shared data source (data/content.js): real article text (CC BY-SA 4.0) + 14 real clips. YouTube thumbnails/embeds load directly; TikTok thumbnails are signed URLs that may not load — they fall back to a gradient.

Static HTML using Tailwind (CDN). Opens directly via file:// — the shared content.js loads as a relative script, and YouTube iframes/thumbnails work.
