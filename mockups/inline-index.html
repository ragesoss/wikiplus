wiki+ Inline concept — 5 plus-side explorations

All five share one skeleton: a real Wikipedia-styled article (serif title/headings, blue wikilinks, real Commons images) as the main column, a sticky “+plus” infobox + Table of Contents with per-section video counts (starting with a special General entry), and a General carousel of whole-topic videos right after the lede. They differ only in the “plus” visual language — colorful, playful, short-form-video-flavored, in deliberate contrast to the scholarly wiki side. Accessibility (contrast, focus, keyboard, text-labeled signals) is the baseline.

Round-3 (all-10) gallery still lives at index.html.
