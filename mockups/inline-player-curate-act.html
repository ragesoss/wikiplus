Action-row anatomy

Why this layout

Metadata sits at top (caption + CC BY-SA credit). The video frame is the hero. The action row anchors the bottom — the natural "I've watched, now decide" position, and it never overlays the video. Curate leads (left, brand, grows); Not relevant trails (right, white, intrinsic width).

Post-action behavior

Curate → curate flow

Opens CurateModal over the dock (logged out → login gate first). The modal's focus trap governs; the dock waits behind it.

Not relevant → optimistic hide + advance

The candidate is hidden instantly (rollback on write failure). The dock then advances to the next suggestion if one exists, else closes. Focus returns to the General band heading.
